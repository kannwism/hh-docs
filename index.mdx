## What is AgentMail?

AgentMail is an API platform that gives AI agents their own inboxes to send, receive, and act upon emails. Enable your agents to communicate through the universal email protocol with services, humans, and other agents.

  <Accordion icon="robot" title="Event-Driven Agents">
